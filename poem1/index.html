The Enchanted Forest

In an enchanted forest, where tall trees sway,
Animals gather to dance and play.

Beneath the canopy where sunlight gleams,
Squirrels chatter and chase their dreams.

Fairies twirl in a magical ballet,
Leaves become their partners in the fey.

The mushrooms hum a lullaby,
As the gentle breeze sighs, oh so high.

Gnomes with lanterns, small and stout,
Light the paths that twist about.

Owls in the night with eyes so bright,
Share stories with the pale moonlight.

Unicorns graze in meadows of green,
Their coats shimmering, a sight unseen
