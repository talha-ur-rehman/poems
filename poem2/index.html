The Magical Garden

In a garden so green, where sunflowers sway,
Magical creatures come out to play.

Beneath the rainbow, where fairies sing,
Butterflies giggle on gossamer wing.

Pixies paint flowers with colors so bright,
In the soft, silver glow of the moon's soft light.

Giggling gnomes with hats so tall,
Tiptoe 'round to see it all.

The wise old owl hoots in delight,
As stars twinkle and dance through the night.

Unicorns graze on candy-floss dreams,
Moonbeams sparkle in chocolate streams.

Dragons blow bubbles that float in the air,
With whispers of secrets only they share.
